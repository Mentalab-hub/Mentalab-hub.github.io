User Guide

To get up and running quickly, please consult the Quick Start Guide included with your Mentalab Explore system, or watch the video below.

Before recording

  1. Before recording EEG data, please do consult our EEG Checklist. This will ensure you get the best performance out of your system.

  2. Working with dry electrodes can be challenging. As such, we compiled a dry electrode guide to help you setup and improve the impedances of your dry electrodes.

  3. We have a host of software APIs for you to integrate with your Explore system. Our flagship API is explorepy, a Python library. To install explorepy, please consult our explorepy Installation Guide or use our Windows installer. For an overview of explorepy commands, please see our documentation, or watch our short tutorials on YouTube.

During recording

To aid your research, we’ve created some Mentalab Explore cheat sheets, which you can refer to as you experiment. One is an LED light guide, which explains how the Explore device will communicate with you. The other is a button command table, which you can use to control the Explore device.

After recording

To maximise the lifespan of your ExG system, it is important to prevent damage to your equipment.

We have compiled instructions on caring for your Mentalab Hardware to help you do this.

Advanced Resources

For users with advanced software needs, check out our GitHub Repo!

All our APIs are open-source and well documented. For more information, please contact us at: support@mentalab.com.
